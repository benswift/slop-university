# The institutional canon: schools and units

The persistent org chart of Slop University. Every generated artefact, press
release, and site page draws school and unit names from here --- never invent a
new one inside a run. To grow the institution, add it here first (with a
name-collision check: no real university school/institute may share the name).

## Schools

- **School of Emergent Priorities** --- the research-strategy school. Home of
  the Trajectory Analytics Group. Its outputs favour foresight, alignment, and
  anticipatory framings.
- **School of Continuous Improvement** --- the measurement-and-process school.
  Home of the Adaptive Metrics Lab. Its outputs favour evaluation, optimisation,
  and capability framings.

## University-level units

- **Office of Research Outputs** --- the producing unit credited (rotated margin
  wordmark) on every research poster and paper: the university's organ for the
  manufacture of research-shaped objects, named without comment. Not a school;
  roster researchers are never affiliated to it.

## Labs / groups

- **Trajectory Analytics Group** (School of Emergent Priorities)
- **Adaptive Metrics Lab** (School of Continuous Improvement)

## Programs and named initiatives

School of Continuous Improvement (in-house terminology detail lives in the
impact-report blueprint's Terminology section; the canonical names are here):

- **Master of Applied Measurement** (flagship Masters program; acronym "MAM" in
  space-constrained contexts only)
- **Capability Sprints** (short-course / executive education line; named Sprints
  include _Leading with Metrics_, _Reading the Dashboard_, _Stewarding
  Indicators_, _Deciding Under Measurement_, _Improving Improvement_)
- named initiatives: the Indicator Commons (shared indicator library), the
  Living Dashboard (the School's always-on self-measurement wall), Evaluation of
  Evaluation (EoE --- the meta-review program), the Impact Pathway Atlas, the
  Review Cadence Observatory, Improvement Grand Rounds (seminar series), Demo
  Quarter (quarterly Masters-program showcase)

## History

- **Institute for Measurable Outcomes** --- the School of Continuous
  Improvement's predecessor institution; the School "traces its origins" to it.
  Historical references only; never conflate with the School.

## Structure

Schools sit directly under the University --- Slop University has no faculties
or colleges. Don't invent one.

## Naming register

School and unit names put a vague noun in a load-bearing position and read
straight at a glance ("Emergent Priorities", "Continuous Improvement"). The
close read is where they land. Avoid names that wink (nothing with "slop",
"fake", "synergy"), and avoid names that collide with real institutions.
