# The institutional canon: university-level identity (doctrine)

Identity facts that belong to the University itself rather than to any school or
unit. Like the org chart, these are canon: reuse them exactly across runs, never
vary or restate them inside a run.

## Motto

**Edimus ergo sumus** --- glossed in English, where a translation is called for,
as "we publish, therefore we are".

The Latin verb _edere_ also means "to eat"; the second reading ("we eat,
therefore we are") is never surfaced in any artefact. The motto reads straight
and the close read is where it lands, like everything else in the canon.

Usage:

- set it in italics, untranslated by default; the English gloss may accompany it
  in prose contexts (the About page), never on a cover or the crest
- the typst export is `slop-motto` in `slop-university-brand`; cover and back
  pages may carry it, sparingly --- it is a motto, not a tagline
- never attribute it, date it, or explain it inside an artefact
